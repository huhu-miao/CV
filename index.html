Projects

Python:

Snake Game: The classic snake game. The snake grows longer when eat the food and dies when hit the wall or collision with other parts of itself. Automatically keep tracking of the highest score of all users.

Pong Game: One of the first computer games. The ball keeps bouncing between two paddles controlled by two players.If one player misses the ball, its opponent wins one point.

Turtle Crossing the Road: The turtle controlled by the player tries to cross a busy road of running cars. Once the turtle crosses the road successfully, the player gets one point and enters to the next level where all the cars speed up a little bit.

U.S States Game: A learning tool for all the people who love geography. The player enters the name of the states in the U.S in the prompt window, it will show on the map if it does exist. When the player want to exits the game, a csv file will be generated to show the player what states he need to learn more.

Hirst Painting: Replicate the work of the great artist Damien Hirst’s spot painting. Extract colors from the original work of the artist and generate random colorful spots on a 500 x 500 pixels screen.

Huntington’s Disease Detecto: Analyze a DNA string for Huntington’s disease and produce a diagnosis. Read the genetic sequence from the file and count the number of CAG repeats.

Kernel filter: An image-processing program that applies various  kernel filters to images, such as Gaussian blur, sharpen, Laplacian, emboss, and motion blur. A kernel filter modifies the pixels in an image by replacing each pixel with a linear combination of its neighboring pixels.
